Featured Artist

Julie Lluch Dalena

Born 1946, Philippines

Empathetic visual art

About the Artist

Lluch is best known for sculpture — working primarily in terracotta, ceramic, stone, and bronze.

She holds a degree in Philosophy from the University of Santo Tomas (UST), Manila.

Artist Statement

"I tell visual stories by pushing art into new sleights that are ethically aware – satire, paradox, even wit – and make the viewer see ethics at work in image."

Selected Exhibitions

  • 3rd Asian Art Show (1989)
  • First Asia-Pacific Triennial (1993)
  • Cultural Center of the Philippines held a retrospective exhibit dedicated to her work (2008)

Awards & Recognition

Thirteen Artists Award by CCP, recognizing emerging artists.

Outstanding Citizenship in Art” from the City of Iligan (1992)

Most Outstanding Woman Artist” from the City of Quezon City (1995)

Sining at Kalinangan Award sa Araw ng Maynila” from the City of Manila (1997)
