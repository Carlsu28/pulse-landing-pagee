Featured Artist

Alvaro Jimenez

Born 1948, Philippines

Septuagenarian Visual Artist

About the Artist

He is largely self-taught as a professional painter; he developed a distinctive style focusing on Filipino life, culture, and everyday scenes — often with a nostalgic or heritage-preserving bent.

He studied at the Don Honorio Ventura Memorial School of Arts and Trades (Bacolor, Pampanga) with a major in Architectural Drafting / Industrial Education.

Artist Statement

"Jimenez has said his goal (or “hangad”) is “to preserve Philippine (Filipino) culture through art."

Selected Exhibitions

  • Kalalangang Kapampangan (2021)
  • Arte Kapampangan group exhibit
