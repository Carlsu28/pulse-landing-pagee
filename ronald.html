Featured Artist

Ronald Ventura

Born 1973, Philippines

Realism Art

About the Artist

He works in layers layering imagery, styles, symbols. These layers may include realism, cartoons, graffiti, historical/religious iconography.

Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA), major in Painting, from University of Santo Tomas (UST).

Artist Statement

"I tell visual stories by pushing art into new sleights that are ethically aware – satire, paradox, even wit – and make the viewer see ethics at work in image."

Selected Exhibitions

  • Japan International Exhibition – Interpretations (2021 – 2023)
  • The Fancy Circus (2020)
  • Recent Works (2015)
  • Ronald Ventura at The National Art of the Philippines (2014)

Awards & Recognition

Winner, 13th ASEAN Sculpture Art Exhibition (Open Competition), Japan (2008)

Finalist, Metrobank Art and Design Excellence (2007)

Awardee, Cultural Center of the Philippines (2003)
